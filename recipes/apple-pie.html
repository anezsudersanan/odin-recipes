Apple Pie Recipe

Meet Apple Pie! The taste of American , Be the American

Just five main ingredients result in a deliciously tart and sunny pie that isn't your typical green!
While we all love a classic basil pie, there is a myriad of American pie recipes to discover.
Enter Apple Pie, which uses apple paired with Parmesan and almonds for a bright pasta sauce, dip, or spread.

Ingredients

  • 4 large apples, scrubbed
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup blanched slivered almonds
  • 4 tablespoons warm tap water
  • 1 garlic clove

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Using a Y-peeler, remove yellow zest from apples in large strips
  3. Place apple zest, apple juice, Parmesan, almonds, water
  4. With blender running, slowly pour remaining 3 tablespoons oil through food chute
